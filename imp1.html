A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Former President Of India

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam BR was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering.

Born:15 October 1931,Rameswaram

Died:27 July 2015, Shilong

Awards:Bharat Ratna, Veer Savarkar Award, Hoover Medal, Padma Bhushan,Padma Vibhushan, and etc.

Education:Madras Institute of Technology,Anna University(1955-1957),

Institute of technology in Chennai,Tamil Nadu

St.Joseph's College(Autonamous),Tiruchirappalli(1946-1954)

College in Tiruchirappalli,Tamil Nadu

Schwartz Higher Secondary School(1946-1950)

Mandapam Panchayat Union Middle School.

Previous offices:President of India(2002-2007),Principal Scientific Adviser to the Government of India.

Parents:Jainulabiddin Marakayar,Ashiamma Jainulabiddin.

Full name: Avul Pakir Jainulabiddin Abdul Kalam.
